Sticker

Composition: Stiker is a blend of surface –active agent and co-adjuvant with detergent .

Benefits:

1 Stiker helps to accelerate spreading on leaf surface
2 Improves wetting and spreading quality of spray solution.
3 Enhances the duration of product efficacy.
4 Stiker is a unique carrier to make available more chemical inside the leaf tissue.

Stiker is safe to use with all type of nutrients spray, micronutrients, insecticides, fungicides and weedicides.

Method of use : 3-5 ml . Stiker is to be mixed with per 10 liter of spray solution and apply

FOR AGRICULTURAL USE ONLY
