Poshan Plus Granules:

It is granulated organic fertilizer with natural Bio-activator and Bio-stimulant for soil application.
The Granules are found in three color i.e. dark brown, green and red.  The maximum moisture content is 1.5%.

Composition:

Humic acid 6% (Min)
Amino acid 3.5% (Min)
Calcium Bentonite 100%
