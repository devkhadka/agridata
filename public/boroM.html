Boro-M

Boro-M is a unique foliar formulation containing Boron compounded with organic solvent (Ethanolamine) and enriched with Molybdenum. It is highly concentrated solution, specially designed to be applied in order to prevent and correct all sign of Boron and Molybdenum deficiencies with regards to cereals, vegetables, citrus fruits trees, seed bearing fruit trees, Tea horticultural produce as well as other affected crops.

Ingredients:

Boron Ethanolamine   12%
Molybdenum        0.05%
Nitrogen  4.00%

Application:

Dissolve 1 ml Boro-M in 1 Lit water and mix thoroughly and spray 1-2 times between the aperture of the leaves buds and after the beginning of fruit formation.

For proper application follow the literature BORO-M should not be mixed with strong alkaline chemicals or agro chemicals.

Caution:

Keep out of the reach of children. maintain out of reach of contact with Food stuffs, beverages and animal feed.  

FOR AGRICULTURAL USE ONLY
