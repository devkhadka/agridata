Poshan

POSHAN is a natural Bioactivetor & biostimulant, contains hydrolysed protein derived from organic source, aminoacids, auxin precursor, cytokinines, sea-weed extracts and essential trace elements.

POSHAN is a foliar spray ,catalyzes growth during vegetable to reproductive stages and accelerates higher utilisation of nutrients in available form results higher yield of quality crops .

Application: 1 ml Poshan to be mixed with 1 lit. of water ,thus 300 lit. solution can be prepared and sprayed over 1 acre of standing crops.

Caution: Do not mix Poshan with other pesticides or fungicides for better result. Use the solution immediate after preparation. After spraying, wash hands and contaminated parts of the body and sprayers as well.
