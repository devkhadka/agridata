AGRINOL PLUS

 

Composition:

Triacontanol  A.I. 0.05% w/w (min). Amino acid 3.0%, Emulsifiers (Ionic or anionic alkyl arl sulphonate and polyoxyethylene ether) 5.0% w/w carrier Q.s., Total: 100% w/w

 

Method of application: 0.5 ml per 1 liter water.

 

First aid: If ingested induce vomiting.

 

Precautions:

Avoid contact with skin or eyes.

Keep away from feed stuffs, children & empty food stuff containers & animal feed.

Store in cool or dry place.

Keep out of reach of children.
