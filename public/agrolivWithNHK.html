AGROLIV with NPK

AGROLIV with NPK is a multi micronutrient foliar formulation enriched with Nitrogen, Phosphorus and Potash (NPK). It helps in producing growth hormones in plants, Nitrogen transformation & Nitrogen fixation for optimum growth and higher yield of crops like cereals, oilseeds, pulses, vegetables, fruits and other crops.

 

Application:

 

Foliar spray @ 2-2.5 ml per Lit. of water. Dissolve 600-750 ml AGROLIV with NPK in 300 Lit. water and spray over 1.5 Acre(approx) of standing crops. Both the surfaces of leaves should be drenched properly. Repeat 2 to 3 sprays at 15-20 days interval.

 

Nutrients contains on percentage basis:

Zinc   6% 
Boron 0.3% 
Iron 0.5%     
Phosphorus 3%
Copper   0.5%
Manganese 0.2%
Nitrogen 2.5%
Potassium 3%
Molybdenum 0.03%

  

AGROLIV with NPK can be used with all type of pesticides of fungicides expect chemicals that produce alkalinity.

 

Caution: Used the solution immediate after preparation. After spraying wash hands and contaminated parts of the body and sprayers as well. Keep out of the reach of children.
