Agrizinc plus

Agrizinc plus  is a light yellow color powdered micronutrient mixer for soil application. It is soluble in water and the moisture content is not more than 1%.

 

Composition:

Zinc (Zn) 8% w/w
Boron (B) 1% w/w
Iron (Fe)        1% w/w
Copper (Cu) 1% w/w
Manganese (Mn) 0.5% w/w
Molybdenum (Mo) 0.03% w/w
Magnesium (Mg) 1% w/w
Sulphur (S) 10% w/w
