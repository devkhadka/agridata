Agrinol

Composition: Triacontanol A.I. 0.05% w/w(min), Emulsifiers (Ionic and Anionic Alkyl Aryl Sulphonate and Polyoxyethylene ether) 0.05% w/w Aromatic solvent Q.S. Total: 100% w/w

First aid : if ingested induce Vomiting.

Cereals :1st spray 3 to 4 weeks after sowing of transplantation.2nd spray 2 to 3 weeks after 1st spray.

Treatment : Symptomatic treatment .keep out of reach of children.

Precautions : Store in cold, dry place. Keep away from feed stuffs, children & empty food stuff containers & animal feedd
Avoid contact with skin & eyes.
