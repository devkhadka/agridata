Calmin-B

Calmin-B is a foilar formulation of Calcium and Boron fortified with physiologically fot the prevention and correction of deficient states due to deficiencies and imbalances in Calcium and Boron assimilation. Clamin-B can be applied as folder application on crops like, oilseeds, potato, tomato, cabbage, cauliflower, brinjal, radish, ginger and fruits like apple, mango, pumpkin, water melon, papaya and cucurbits.


Method of application:

2-5 gm per lit.of water depending upon the deficiences of calcium and boron in crops.

Calmin-B is compatible with all agrochemicals and fertilizers except products containing copper, sulphur and alkaline products.

Nutrient contents by percentage(Minimum):

Calcium

 10% w/w
Boron 2% w/w
Fortified with L-Aminoacids And Nitrogen Carrier 100% w/w


Note:

Use within 3 yrs form the Date of Mfg.

Keep out of reach of children, food and feed supplements.

Store in dry place.

FOR AGRICULTURAL USE ONLY
