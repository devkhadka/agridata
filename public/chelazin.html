Chelazin(chelated Zinc)

Method of use:

1. Foliar spray: 50 gm of Chelazin(technical) is to be dissolved in 50 lt of water and spray over 0.2 acre(6 katha App.)of standing depending upon the severity of zinc deficiency.

2. Soil application: 250 gm of chelazin is to be mixed with 5 kg soil or compost fertiliser and apply before or after sowing .
